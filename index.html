The King James Bio


Growing Up

James was born on December 30, 1984, in Akron, Ohio to a 16-year-old mother, Gloria Marie James. James started playing organized basketball in the fifth grade. He later played Amateur Athletic Union (AAU) basketball for the Northeast Ohio Shooting Stars.

I Promise School


"Nothing is given. Everything is earned."


The I Promise School (IPS) is a public elementary school in Akron, Ohio, opened in 2018, supported by the LeBron James Family Foundation, and specifically aimed at at-risk children.
